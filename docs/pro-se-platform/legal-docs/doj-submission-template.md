## SECTION 1: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Overview
This criminal referral concerns a systematic scheme of mail fraud, wire fraud, and ERISA violations orchestrated by Charles Schwab Corporation and its subsidiary, Sedgwick Claims Management Services, Inc. The scheme involves the fraudulent denial and mishandling of employee benefit claims affecting thousands of claimants across multiple states, constituting a pattern of racketeering activity under 18 U.S.C. § 1962.
