### 2. SEC FORM TCR (TIPS, COMPLAINTS, AND REFERRALS)

**Deadline:** 30 days from first SEC submission for award eligibility
**Statute:** Securities Exchange Act Section 21F

#### Why File This
- **Whistleblower Award:** 10-30% of sanctions recovered by SEC
  - Example: $10M recovery = up to $3M award
- **Additional Protection:** SEC Rule 21F-17(a) prohibits interference
- **Official Record:** Documents your disclosure for potential enforcement action

#### How to File

### What to Say (If Asked)
- "I'm involved in a legal matter and cannot comment."
- "My attorney has advised me not to discuss this." (even if pro se)
- "I've filed administrative complaints and cannot discuss pending matters."
- **DO NOT** discuss on social media
- **DO NOT** discuss with former colleagues

### What NOT to Do
- [ ] Do NOT post about case on social media
- [ ] Do NOT discuss with former colleagues (witness tampering risk)
- [ ] Do NOT contact Schwab/Sedgwick personnel
- [ ] Do NOT destroy or alter evidence
- [ ] Do NOT threaten anyone
- [ ] Do NOT try to "build a case" by improper methods

### If Contacted by Schwab's Attorneys
- "I decline to speak with you."
- Refer them to your attorney (if represented)
- If pro se: "All communication must be in writing."
- **DO NOT** engage in conversation
- **Document** all contact attempts

### If Contacted by Government Investigators
- **DO NOT** lie or mislead (federal crime)
- **You CAN** speak with them (your choice)
- Consider: "I'd like to consult with an attorney before answering questions."
- **Be truthful** and complete
- **Document** all interactions
